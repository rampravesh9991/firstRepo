Chemical Elements

The periodic table is a tabular arrangement of chemical elements. Each element is represented by its chemical symbol, such as Fe for iron, H for hydrogen, O for Oxygen. Elements are classified into groups and periods based on their properties.

Chemical Equations

Chemical reactions are represented using Chemical equations. Here is an example of a chemical reaction

2H2 + O2 -> 2H2O

This equations represents the reaction between hydrogen gas (h2) and Oxygen gas (O2) to form (H2O)

Important Concepts

Stoichiometry is the calculation of reactants and products in the chemical reaction. It involves balancing chemical equation to ensure mass and charge are conserved. Subscripts indicate the number of atom of each element in a molecule, while Superscripts represents charges or number of atoms in an ion.
